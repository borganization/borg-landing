Features

  • Runs on your machine
  • Security focused
  • Long term / short term memory
  • Evolves as you use it
  • Self-healing
  • Plugin system to extend capabilities
  • Works with Telegram, Slack, Teams, iMessage, and more
  • Model agnostic

Borg evolves — Your base Borg evolves to a specialized Borg

You start with a base Borg. Each tool call, task, and interaction earns (or loses) XP, builds a bond, and drives evolution. Over time, your Borg becomes a unique specialist shaped by your usage, preferences, and chosen path.
